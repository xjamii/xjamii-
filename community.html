Community Standards

X-Jamii Community Standards

Our standards outline what is and isn't allowed on Aviationin. They're designed to ensure all members can participate freely and safely in our aviation community.

Safety

No Threats or Violence

We do not allow content that threatens, incites, or promotes violence against individuals or groups. This includes threats related to aviation safety or operations.

Personal Safety

Do not post content that reveals private personal information (doxing) or coordinates harassment. Aviation professionals' sensitive information must be protected.

Authenticity

Authentic Identity

Members must represent themselves truthfully. Impersonation of aviation professionals, airlines, or aviation organizations is strictly prohibited.

No Misinformation

Do not share false or misleading information about aviation safety, procedures, or operations that could endanger others.

Professional Conduct

Respectful Communication

Maintain professional discourse. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discriminatory language based on race, ethnicity, gender, or other protected characteristics is not tolerated.

Confidential Information

Do not share confidential aviation procedures, security protocols, or proprietary airline information that could compromise safety or operations.

Content Guidelines

Adult Content

X-Jamii is a professional network. Nudity, pornography, and sexually explicit content are not permitted.

Illegal Activities

Content promoting illegal activities, including unauthorized drone operations, aviation violations, or other unlawful behavior is prohibited.

Commercial Content

Unsolicited advertising, spam, or repetitive promotional content is not allowed unless in designated commercial areas.

Enforcement

Reporting

Members can report violations through our reporting system. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team.

Consequences

Violations may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent banning from X-Jamii, depending on severity and context.

Appeals

Members may appeal enforcement actions if they believe a mistake was made. Contact our support team with relevant details.

Last updated: July 2025 | Aviationin reserves the right to update these standards as needed.
