Account Settings

How to update my profile information

To update your profile:

  1. Tap your profile picture in the top right corner
  2. Select "Edit Profile"
  3. Make changes to any field (name, bio, profile picture, etc.)
  4. Tap "Save Changes" when finished

Note: Some changes like username may require admin approval if they've been recently modified.

Changing my password

To change your password:

  1. Go to Settings > Account > Password
  2. Enter your current password
  3. Enter your new password (must be at least 8 characters)
  4. Confirm your new password
  5. Tap "Update Password"

You'll receive an email confirmation when your password is successfully changed.

Verification process

Aviationin offers three verification levels:

  • Basic Verification: Email and phone number confirmation
  • Professional Verification: Upload aviation credentials (license, ID badge)
  • Premium Verification: Manual review by Aviationin team (for airlines and organizations)

To start verification:

  1. Go to your profile
  2. Tap "Get Verified"
  3. Select verification level
  4. Upload required documents
  5. Submit for review

Professional verification typically takes 2-3 business days.

Connections

How to connect with other professionals

There are several ways to connect:

  • Search: Use the search bar to find colleagues by name, airline, or position
  • Suggestions: Check "People You May Know" on your home feed
  • QR Code: Share your unique Aviationin QR code at events
  • Groups: Join aviation groups and connect with members

When sending a connection request, we recommend including a personal note about how you know the person or why you'd like to connect.

Managing connection requests

To manage your connection requests:

  1. Tap the "Connections" icon in the bottom navigation
  2. Select "Requests"
  3. For each request you can:
    • Accept (immediately connects you)
    • Ignore (declines without notification)
    • Report (for suspicious requests)

You can also set your account to automatically accept connections from people in your:

  • Current airline
  • Flight school alumni
  • Verified aviation organizations

Connection privacy settings

Control who can see your connections:

  • Public: Anyone can see your connections
  • Connections Only: Only your connections can see your network
  • Private: No one can see your connections

To adjust these settings:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy > Connections
  2. Select your preferred visibility
  3. Tap "Save"

Note: Even with private settings, your connections may appear in mutual connection lists.

Aviation Features

Using the digital logbook

Aviationin's digital logbook helps you:

  • Track flight hours by aircraft type
  • Record landings and approaches
  • Generate reports for interviews or audits
  • Backup your flight records

To add flights:

  1. Go to Logbook > Add Flight
  2. Enter flight details (date, aircraft, duration, etc.)
  3. Add optional notes or documents
  4. Save

You can import flights from:

  • ForeFlight
  • Garmin Pilot
  • CSV files

Adding certifications

To add your aviation certifications:

  1. Go to your profile > Certifications
  2. Tap "Add Certification"
  3. Select certification type (PPL, CPL, ATP, type ratings, etc.)
  4. Enter certificate number and expiration date
  5. Upload scan of your certificate (optional but recommended for verification)
  6. Save

Certification badges will appear on your profile. Verified certifications show a checkmark.

Note: You can set certifications to:

  • Public (visible to everyone)
  • Connections Only
  • Private (only visible to you)

Job board features

The Aviationin job board helps you:

  • Find pilot, crew, and aviation maintenance positions
  • Apply directly through your profile
  • Get matched with suitable positions

Key features:

  • Smart Matching: Get alerts when jobs match your qualifications
  • One-Click Apply: Use your Aviationin profile to apply
  • Airline Insights: See employee reviews and hiring trends

To access the job board:

  1. Tap "Jobs" in the bottom navigation
  2. Browse or search for positions
  3. Use filters to narrow results (location, aircraft type, etc.)

Privacy & Security

Adjusting privacy settings

Control your privacy on Aviationin:

  • Profile Visibility: Who can see your profile (public, connections only, or private)
  • Activity Broadcasts: Choose what gets shared to your network (new connections, certifications, etc.)
  • Search Visibility: Appear in search results on and off Aviationin
  • Data Sharing: Control how your data is used for personalization

To adjust settings:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy
  2. Select each category to customize
  3. Tap "Save" after making changes

Reporting inappropriate content

To report content or users:

  1. Tap the three dots (...) on any post or profile
  2. Select "Report"
  3. Choose a reason (harassment, misinformation, etc.)
  4. Add optional details
  5. Submit

Our moderation team reviews reports within 24 hours. You'll receive an update when action is taken.

Emergency: For immediate safety concerns, contact aviation authorities directly.

Two-factor authentication

Enable 2FA for extra security:

  1. Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication
  2. Choose your method:
    • Text Message: Receive codes via SMS
    • Authenticator App: Use Google Authenticator or similar
  3. Follow setup instructions
  4. Save backup codes in a secure location

Note: 2FA is required for:

  • Airline administrator accounts
  • Accounts with verification badges
  • Users accessing sensitive aviation data
