Cookies Policy

How Aviationin uses cookies and similar technologies in our aviation professional network

About Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our aviation professional network. Like most aviation service platforms, we use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Ensure secure access for aviation professionals
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve platform performance and safety
  • Analyze how aviation professionals use our services
  • Deliver relevant aviation industry content

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for secure authentication and core platform functionality Session or up to 24 hours
Preference Cookies Remember your professional settings and display preferences Up to 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how aviation professionals use our platform Up to 2 years
Security Cookies Protect against unauthorized access and maintain aviation data security Up to 6 months

Third-Party Cookies

We work with carefully selected aviation industry partners who may set cookies when you use our platform:

  • Analytics Providers: To help us improve our services for aviation professionals
  • Security Services: To protect against threats to aviation data
  • Embedded Content: From trusted aviation industry resources

We do not allow third-party advertising cookies on our aviation professional network.
